Who We Are?

Retro&Demure Vintage is a one-stop shop for authentic vintage clothing, carefully curated to bring timeless styles back to life. Founded in 1998, we have been dedicated to preserving fashion's golden eras by offering unique clothing and accessories that tell a story. From retro to classic, each piece embodies the essence of a bygone era.

We believe in sustainable fashion and the beauty of nostalgia, handpicking each item to bring you a true vintage experience.
